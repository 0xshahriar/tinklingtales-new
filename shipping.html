Shipping & delivery

We partner with reliable carriers to ensure your bangles reach safely and on time.

Domestic (Bangladesh)

  • Dhaka metro: 1-2 business days via local courier partners
  • Rest of Bangladesh: 3-5 business days with Sundarban or SA Paribahan
  • Cash on delivery available in Dhaka and Chattogram

International

  • Tracked shipping via DHL, Aramex and FedEx
  • Delivery timeline: 7-14 business days
  • Duties and taxes payable by recipient

Order tracking

  • Tracking IDs are shared via email and SMS once your bangles leave our studio.
  • Need help? Message us on Messenger or WhatsApp with your order ID for real-time support.
