Our story

Crafting memories, one clink at a time

Tinkling Tales began in Dhaka as a passion project to celebrate Bangladeshi artistry. Today, we partner with metal and glass artisans across Old Dhaka and Chattogram to bring thoughtfully designed bangles that blend tradition with modern aesthetics.

We believe jewellery should narrate your journey. Every stack is assembled by hand, inspected for quality and packaged sustainably for celebrations across Bangladesh and beyond.
