Our Journey

From a living room studio to beloved trousseaus worldwide

A humble beginning

Founder Naina started Tinkling Tales as a heartfelt experiment to revive the sound of traditional bangles in everyday wardrobes. Friends and family became the first muses, requesting custom stacks for milestone celebrations.

Celebrating community

Today we collaborate with artisans across Jaipur and Hyderabad, weaving their expertise into modern silhouettes. Each limited drop supports fair wages, sustainable sourcing, and skill preservation.

What Matters

Values that guide every shimmering stack

Intentional Design

Each set is curated to layer effortlessly, pairing textures and finishes that harmonise without overpowering your personal style.

Responsible Craft

We prioritise skin-safe alloys, recycled metals, and packaging made from cotton rag paper to reduce our environmental footprint.

Personal Connections

Styling consultations help us translate your milestones into wearable keepsakes, whether it is a wedding, festival, or first day at a new job.

Behind the Scenes

Meet the hands that shape each chime

Design Studio

Sketching, prototyping, and testing each silhouette for comfort and durability before it leaves our Mumbai studio.

Metal Smiths

Seasoned craftsmen who forge and polish bangles, ensuring smooth edges and luxurious finishes in every batch.

Embellishment Artists

Detail-oriented artisans applying kundan, meenakari, and pearl work, balancing sparkle with refined restraint.

Packaging Partners

Local women-led collectives that assemble our keepsake boxes and handwritten notes for every order.
